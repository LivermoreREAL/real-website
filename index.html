About REAL

A Quality Networking Experience for the Livermore Real Estate Community

The Real Estate Alliance of Livermore creates a space where Realtors, affiliates, and professionals can connect, learn, and build real relationships. We feature current listings on our weekly Broker Tour, host quality guest speakers, and announce community events.

Most importantly, we have fun, network, and build the relationships that drive business in the Tri-Valley.

✓ Weekly guest speakers
✓ Broker tour listings
✓ Super Tour cash prizes
✓ Community announcements
✓ Panel discussions

Good to Know

Meeting Policies

📢 Networking Etiquette

Networking is encouraged, but please refrain from openly soliciting products and services during the meeting. The room is open for networking at 8:30 AM and after the meeting until 10:00 AM.

📄 Flyer Policy

Only the current week's speaker or a paid sponsor presenting that day may place flyers or materials on tables. Others are welcome to share during Community Announcements and distribute outside after the meeting.
